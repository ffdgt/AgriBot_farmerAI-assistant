Facts & Info

💧
Paddy Cultivation: Rice needs about 1,500 liters of water to produce one kilogram of grain. Efficient water management is key!
🛡️
Pesticide Safety: Always wear protective gear when handling pesticides. Over 95% of sprayed herbicides can miss their target, affecting the environment.
🌱
Soil Health: Healthy soil is alive! A single teaspoon can contain billions of microbes. Using compost and organic matter feeds these essential organisms.
🐝
Pollinators: Bees are crucial for many crops. Avoid spraying insecticides during peak bee activity (usually mid-day) to protect them.
